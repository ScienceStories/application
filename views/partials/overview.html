Highlighting 5 Women at Yale University in Scientific Research Before Undergraduate Co-education.

Katharine Jeanette Bush (1855-1937)
Zoologist & Researcher
First woman to receive a Ph.D. in the sciences at Yale University
Grace Hopper (1906-1992)
Computer Scientist & U.S. Navy Rear Admiral
Pioneer of computer programming and inventor of early compiler tools
Dorrit Hoffleit (1907-2007)
Research Astronomer
Inventor of the Bright Star Catalog
Maxine Singer (1931-)
Molecular Biologist
Contributor to research on solving the genetic code in DNA
Dorothy Horstmann (1911-2001)
Epidemiologist, Virologist and Pediatrician
First woman appointed as a professor at the Yale School of Medicine
